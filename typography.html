Typography

For our typography, we used both sans-serif fonts, Poppins and Signika. Poppins font is mostly used in our main headings and buttons with a bold style for emphasis. Signika font has a narrow and tall x-height to improve the readability of texts in small sizes. These fonts pair well together because of the contrast with width. Both Poppins and Signika have roundness in their type which evokes the feeling of friendliness without diminishing its readability.
